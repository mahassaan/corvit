Who are we

Corvit Systems has always ventured to provide the latest and the most relevant Information Technology (IT) education to the students and the professionals of the region at a remarkably affordable cost. As the leading provider of technical education and services to the conventional and corporate market, we focus on delivering quality customized IT education. The training division comprises skilled instructors with practical knowledge of their respective fields, to give the students real world exposure. Our training courses are designed to suit the need of existing as well as aspiring IT professionals. We offer a wide variety of trainings on various products, covering most of the modern day technologies from vendors like Cisco, Microsoft, Red Hat and Sun etc.
